The Intelligence Behind StudyWiz

StudyWiz uses heuristic AI logic where subjects with higher difficulty receive more study sessions. The system distributes workload evenly across the week to maintain balance.

1. Problem Modeling

Subjects are treated as tasks and difficulty defines priority weight.

2. AI Scheduling

Weighted allocation distributes study sessions intelligently across days.

3. Performance Prediction

Performance is estimated using weighted scoring based on workload.
